Category B (30% Seats) The remaining 30% of seats are filled by the Institute under the Management/NRI quota as per APSCHE and Government of Andhra Pradesh guidelines. 3 Lateral Entry (10% Additional) The ECET convener admits 10% of the sanctioned intake directly into the second year of the 4-year B.Tech program from among Diploma holders, based on their ECET rank and reservation policy.
