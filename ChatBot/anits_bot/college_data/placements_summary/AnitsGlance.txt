Information Technology Mechanical Engineering MBA (Hospital Administration) Basic Sciences & Humanities BS&H-HOD Chemistry English and Humanities Mathematics Physics R&D R&D IQAC Policies Facilities Library Training Placements About ANITS Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology and Sciences (ANITS) was established in the academic year 2001-02 with approval from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and the Government of Andhra Pradesh. It is permanently affiliated to Andhra University, Visakhapatnam. ANITS offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology, management, focusing on academic excellence and industry relevance. The institute provides modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and research-driven learning. Accredited by NBA and recognized by UGC, ANITS promotes innovation, discipline, and holistic student development. It stands as a premier institution in the region, preparing students for global careers and challenges.
